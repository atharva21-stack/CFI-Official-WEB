Kill Your Trigger Points

Triggers are a little-known key to forming a new habit (or breaking an old one). A trigger is an event that will kick off that automatic urge to do a habit.
Like for smokers they have a number of triggers — when they drink alcohol or coffee, many smokers will have a urge to smoke.

Similarly, identify your trigger spots that cause you to crave your addictions and write them down. Once you've written them down, attempt to keep yourself occupied with anything else during those locations/times so you don't feel compelled to use.

Benifits of Knowing your Trigger point -

  • Easiest Way of Breaking an habit
  • Easily Trackable
  • Fast Results
