who we are About Us

Cancer foundation of India

Kolkata-based Cancer Foundation of India (CFI) is committed to prevent cancer as a means to control cancer in India. With its team of motivated and creative professionals, it endeavors to bring in a paradigm change in the approach toward prevention of cancer. CFI conducts capacity building programmes, publish literature and prepares innovative communication material (IEC) for clinicians and social & health workers.

Prof. Maqsood Siddiqi
