Watch your circle my freind...

" HELLO, Hey! Look at the damage it's done to my tongue!" Exclaims Mr. Sachin Manohar , when he observes today's youth chewing tobacco. “ So , as we say ! “Everything happens for a reason !” One affected , can inspire the youth ! In 2015 , Mr Sachin Manohar Handage got a mouth ulcer and a venous clot near his throat. When he visited the doctor , the doctor said there is a chance of Mouth Cancer . His little brother, being a pharmacist, did an extensive research on cancer treatments and cures, as well as a hunt for cancer treatment health centers where he might find good doctors to treat Mr. Sachin . Mr Sachin gave up tobacco as soon as he was cognizant that it was causing him cancer! After 6 months of radiations and chemotherapies , he eventually gained faith in himself and realized that he had overcome cancer! He had maintained his composure during the entire process and spent roughly 6-7 lakhs for the cancer treatment . He gave up all of his bad habits and addictions, including smoking, drinking, and even eating non-vegetarian food . Unfortunately, his mouth cancer recurred in 2018 i.e after 3 years of the treatment , owing to anxiety , mental stress and daily slog .This time he was under a lot of pressure . It was shocking that despite taking so many measures and receiving treatment at Tata Hospital, he was still diagnosed with cancer . Keeping a positive outlook on the situation which is getting repeated unfortunately, he subsequently began using ayurvedic and homeopathic therapies, which he found effective for his body .
