Know your Reason

You’re thinking of quitting tobcco consumption. Do you know the reasons you want to quit? Do you want to be healthier? Save money? Keep your loved ones safe? Why are there so many reasons?

  • If you’re not sure, ask yourself these questions:
    • Want to look, feel, and be healthier?
    • Want a better lifestyle?
    • Want a better family life?


  • Quitting will make you feel better and improve your health. But there are other reasons to quit that you might not have thought about.

  • Make a list of every reason you want to quit tobcco consumption.

  • Keep the list in a visible location, such as your car or the spot where you used to keep your cigarettes. Take a glance at the list whenever you feel the need to smoke to remind yourself why you want to quit.


Remind yourself of your reasons for quitting every day once you've identified them. It can also motivate you to quit tobcco consumption permanently.
